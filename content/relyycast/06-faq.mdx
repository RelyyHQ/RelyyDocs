# FAQ

## Who this is for

Users who want quick answers to common setup and runtime questions.

## Prerequisites

- Basic familiarity with the `Control` and `Settings` tabs.

## Steps

1. Find your question below.
2. If unresolved, continue to troubleshooting and support links.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does RelyyCast work without Cloudflare?

Yes. You can run local-only mode and stream via local MP3/HLS URLs.

### Why is Cloudflare stuck on `PENDING-CONSENT`?

That is the default state before you click the connect action.

### Why is my MP3 button disabled?

`MP3 Output` is likely disabled in `Settings`.

### Why does my temp URL keep changing?

`trycloudflare.com` URLs rotate when you reconnect.

### Why does HLS not load while runtime seems healthy?

`Relay Path` may not match your URL, or ingest has not started feeding MediaMTX yet.

### Which platforms are supported?

macOS and Windows are supported now. Linux support is planned.

## What success looks like

- You can answer common setup questions without deep debugging.

## Common mistakes

- Treating Cloudflare login prompts as app failures.
- Testing old copied temp URLs after reconnect.
