# Support

## Who this is for

Users who still need help after setup and troubleshooting.

## Prerequisites

- Collect basic issue details before requesting support.

## Steps

1. Capture what you see in the app:
   - Runtime status
   - Cloudflare status
   - Relay status
   - Exact error text, if shown
2. Note your environment:
   - Operating system
   - RelyyCast app version
   - Whether issue affects MP3, HLS, Cloudflare URL, or all outputs
3. Share details in the Relyy community support channel.

## What success looks like

- Support can reproduce your issue quickly and suggest a targeted fix.

## Common mistakes

- Reporting "it does not work" without statuses or error details.
- Omitting whether the issue is local-only or Cloudflare-specific.
