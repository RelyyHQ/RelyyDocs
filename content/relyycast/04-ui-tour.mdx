# UI Tour

## Who this is for

New users who want a quick orientation of where controls and status live.

## Prerequisites

- App installed and opened at least once.

## Steps

1. Open the `Control` tab.
2. Review the `Cloudflare` panel:
   - Mode switch: `Temp URL` or `Custom Domain`
   - Actions: `Start Temp URL`, `Save & Connect`, `Skip for Now`, `Retry`
3. Review the `Status` tiles:
   - `Runtime`
   - `Cloudflare`
   - `Relay`
   - `Listeners`
4. Review the `Stream` section:
   - `MP3` row and `Open MP3`
   - `HLS` row and `Open HLS`
5. Open the `Settings` tab and verify key fields:
   - `MP3 Output`
   - `Input URL`
   - `Station Name`
   - `Bitrate`
   - `Relay Path`
   - `Genre`
   - `Description`
   - `Save Settings`
6. Use the footer badges to monitor runtime quickly:
   - `Phase`
   - `Relay`
   - `CF`

## What success looks like

- You can identify where to configure settings, connect Cloudflare, and copy/open stream URLs.
- You know where to read real-time health indicators.

## Common mistakes

- Treating `pending-consent` as an error; it is expected before connect.
- Assuming `Relay: Pending` means app failure when source ingest is not active yet.
